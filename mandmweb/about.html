About Mood & Mirror

Mood & Mirror is a digital space where style becomes personal. Created by Safiyya Hill, it's a platform built from my own relationship with fashion, how it grounds me, comforts me, and lets me reinvent myself whenever life feels overwhelming or uncertain. Growing up, clothes were my safe way to explore who I wanted to be, and Mood & Mirror brings that same freedom to anyone who visits.

I designed this space for people who don't just "get dressed", but who feel their outfits. People who use color to shift their mood, textures to express their energy, and silhouettes to tell a story without saying a word. With Mood & Mirror, you can visualize your fashion moods, try out unexpected combinations, and experiment with your inner world through color, texture, and form from your own closet.

More than anything, this project reflects my belief that style is deeply emotional. It's not about trends, it's about identity, confidence, and the small transformations we give ourselves every day. Mood & Mirror encourages users to explore who they are, who they were, and who they're becoming, all through the mirror of personal style.
